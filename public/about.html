Welcome to Brasov

Brașov is one of the most charming and picturesque cities in Romania, located in the heart of Transylvania. Known for its medieval architecture and stunning natural surroundings, Brasov attracts visitors from all around the world.

History

Brașov has a rich history dating back to the 13th century, with strong influences from Saxons who helped shape its cultural and architectural heritage.
